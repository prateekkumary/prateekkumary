# 💫 About Me:
My journey in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence began with a simple curiosity - how can data truly make systems smarter and decision-making faster?<br><br>That curiosity soon evolved into a strong passion for uncovering patterns, building intelligent models, and turning raw data into meaningful action. With a background in Computer Science Engineering and ongoing advanced learning in Data Science and AI at IIT Guwahati, I’ve built a foundation rooted in both analytical thinking and hands-on experimentation.<br><br>I enjoy exploring the full data pipeline - from collecting and cleaning data to developing machine learning models, LLM-based agents, and real-time analytical dashboards. For me, every dataset is a story waiting to be told, and every model is a step toward making technology more human-aware.<br><br>What drives me most is the impact: using AI to solve complex, real-world problems across domains like predictive analytics, automation, and intelligent systems. I’m passionate about blending creativity with technical precision - ensuring every project I work on is both insightful and purposeful.
